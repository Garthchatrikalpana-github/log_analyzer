## 🔍 Root Cause Analysis

The root cause of the incident is a `sqlalchemy.exc.ArgumentError` that occurs when querying all records from the `Product` table without explicitly declaring it as a text or column object. The error message indicates that the column expression 'Product' should be explicitly declared with `text('Product')` or `column('Product')`.
