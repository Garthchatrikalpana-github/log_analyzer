## 🔍 Root Cause Analysis

The root cause of the incident is a SQLAlchemy error in the `products` route of the `mini-commerce` application. The error occurs because SQLAlchemy is expecting a textual column expression 'Product' to be explicitly declared with `text('Product')` or `column('Product')` for more specificity.
